LTI Project: Facebook App

I am making a Facebook app for my internship project. The app's purpose is to send notifications about school events, holidays, etc that are relevant to you to your Facebook. Notifications are rather unintrusive, which is why I chose them. This has been a lot harder than it sounds; Facebook has a very robust framework behind the scenes.
